Tracking Alpha in the Fog of Noise

A research architecture for exploring strategy populations in high-dimensional space using vector search and multiple hypothesis tracking.

The Problem

Markets contain vast numbers of candidate strategies. Distinguishing real signal from statistical noise becomes increasingly difficult as dimensionality increases.

  • High candidate populations
  • Weak signal structures
  • Multiple competing hypotheses
  • Time-evolving states

Research Direction

Instead of testing strategies sequentially, we explore candidate populations through vector similarity and hypothesis branching.

MHT-FAISS Engine

  • Strategy vectors encoded into high-dimensional representations
  • FAISS enables fast similarity search
  • MHT maintains evolving candidate branches
  • Agent systems evaluate strategy paths

Interactive Research Surface

The architecture is exposed through a live research interface allowing exploration of candidate clusters and hypothesis branches.
