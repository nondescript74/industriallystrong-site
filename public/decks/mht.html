Tracking Alpha in the Fog of Noise

A research architecture for exploring large populations of candidate strategies using vector similarity search and Multiple Hypothesis Tracking.

The Problem

Financial markets produce vast numbers of candidate strategies. Distinguishing signal from statistical noise becomes increasingly difficult as dimensionality and candidate populations grow.

  • Large strategy populations
  • Weak statistical signals
  • Multiple competing hypotheses
  • Time-evolving market structure

Research Direction

Instead of sequential testing, candidate strategies are embedded into high-dimensional space and explored using similarity search combined with hypothesis branching and pruning.

MHT-FAISS Architecture

  • Strategy vectors in high-dimensional representation space
  • FAISS similarity search across candidate populations
  • Multiple Hypothesis Tracking to maintain competing paths
  • Agent systems evaluating strategy branches

Interactive Research Surface

The architecture is exposed through a live interactive environment allowing exploration of clusters, candidate paths, and evolving hypothesis branches.
