UNDERLYING ARCHITECTURE

State Resolution Architecture

Two systems processing the same asynchronous signal stream — one collapses uncertainty, the other preserves competing candidate states until evidence converges.

Why This Matters for CTA Allocation
In the fog of noise, systems that resolve uncertainty too early misread regime changes and concentration risk. This engine maintains competing candidate states across evaluation cycles, surfacing contradictions and preserving alternatives until evidence converges — then commits. The architecture comparison below shows the structural difference.
