Architecture Selection

Three wavelength regimes sharing a common distributed adaptive writer platform. Click a card or use the dropdown above to switch.

Architecture A — NIR/Visible

Proof-of-concept platform. NIR/visible VCSELs with standard MEMS steering. Validates tiling, stitching, and calibration subsystems.

405 nm (GaN VCSEL) Res: 500 nm Tile: 200 µm NA: 0.3 GaN VCSEL array TRL 5

Architecture B — Deep UV

Production-class DUV writer. AlGaN deep-UV emitters at 193–300 nm with UV-compatible MEMS optics and high-NA micro-objectives.

248 nm (AlGaN DUV) Res: 80 nm Tile: 100 µm NA: 0.6 AlGaN DUV VCSEL array TRL 3

Architecture C — EUV (Rydberg HHG)

EUV prototype. Rydberg-enhanced HHG source feeds MEMS mirror modulator heads with Mo/Si multilayer coatings at 13.5 nm.

13.5 nm (Rydberg HHG) Res: 7 nm Tile: 100 µm NA: 0.33 Mo/Si MEMS mirror modulator TRL 2
