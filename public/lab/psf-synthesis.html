Incoherent PSF Compositing

Physics constraint: In the incoherent regime (intensities add), the composite PSF is always broader than or equal to the native PSF — you cannot sharpen below the native resolution. The value is in reshaping: creating flat-top, annular, or asymmetric dose profiles with superior dose containment and uniformity within the target region.
